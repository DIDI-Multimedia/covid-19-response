Currently, the Covid-19 pandemic brings with it the vital task of social distancing, making such funeral traditions increasingly difficult to achieve. Every nation has lost lives due to the virus and we are all brought together in this grief. Being able to honor and commemorate those lost in the crises is something we felt to be extremely important in this time. They deserve to be remembered, and their loved ones deserve a chance to say goodbye.

What if there was a way to honor these lost lives by humanizing the statistic, and bringing our global society together to share in the loss brought by this pandemic. As our lives have been forced heavily to shift during this time frame, we felt that there was a way to find a solution for this issue by likewise moving this tradition of funerals into a modern platform. We hope to generate an online memorial platform in which people can grieve the loss of loved ones collectively regardless of the distance created by the pandemic.

We hope to use this space to raise awareness of the ongoing issue by creating a data visualization highlighting the human behind the lost lives. In this way, we hope to show how this is an issue that is affecting us globally and spreading quickly.  

We hope to represent these lost lives in this visualization by including any information found and willing to be shared concerning each individual life. Furthermore by creating a visualization to represent the lives lost globally, we are allowing these individuals to be appropriately remembered and to bring each us us together in empathy and healing.
