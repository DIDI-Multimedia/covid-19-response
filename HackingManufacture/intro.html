HACKING MANUFACTURE

We developed easy to manufacture and assemble face shields for healthcare workers. Our design uses the least amount of material possible while simultaneously providing the most protection making it cost effective


THE VISION

To be able to create an efficient, low cost and easy to manufacture face shield model accessible to all. We hope that this model is picked up by manufacturers and distributed amongst hospitals.

We are doing this because we felt the responsibility as designers to provide solutions for the frontliners of this pandemic. We want to contribute our skills for the greater good. We want to give back to the community and UAE. We just want to participate and do our best by being a part of the solution instead of just watching from the sidelines.
