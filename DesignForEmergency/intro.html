CoLive

A digital platform that serves as an online interactive community and emotional data repository.


WHY ARE WE DOING THIS?

Our aim is to give people a platform where they can converse to express their views and share their experiences. In such unprecedented times, it is important to acknowledge that mental health can be drastically affected. We want everyone to know that they are not alone in this. The collective conversations will help build a repository of emotions and experiences faced over the pandemic to narrate this historical moment through the people's perspectives.

THE VISION

Right from the get-go, we've been working on a concept that addresses the ever-growing lack of socialization. The goal is to create deeper connections in spite of not being physically connected. This is achieved by our conversational podcasts that asures everyone that they are not alone in their experiences

STEPS OF THE JOURNEY
